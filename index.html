And if that's not enough, then that's okay. Things are not always going to go our way, but that's okay. Life always has a way of working things out.

  • Realizing I have a lot of work to do for this next week
Rant of the Day:

Today was a pretty relaxed day. I kind of feel guilty for taking a break for the day after taking my CSE 101 quiz, but I need to remember that sometimes it's okay to just take a day for yourself. We can't keep grinding 24/7 or else we will just burn out completely. I enjoyed just spending some time to myself and doing something that wasn't school or CS related. Sometimes, I wish that I could enjoy doing CS related things on my free time, but I just really need a break from it. I wonder how some people gain the motivation and drive to spend their free time coding or doing personal projects. I wish I could be like that. But sometimes I really just feel like I want to spend my last remaining moments as a college student in a fun way. I don't want to remember my college experience as me just continually worrying about my future and my career because before I know it, I'll be graduating and working for the rest of my life. It's a sad thought to think about, but I'm hopeful that there will be some good things to happen in the future too.
